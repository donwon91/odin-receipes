Steps to Greatness

Making ramen is difficult and the broth is the hardest part. But follow these instuctions and you too can make awesome Ramen!

  1. Bring Tonkotsu to a simmer
  2. As broth simmers, heat up until soft to bite but soggy the veggies
  3. Once veggies are done, soft boil egg
  4. Cook the egg noodles, 5 minutes ago.
  5. Bring it all together and nom
