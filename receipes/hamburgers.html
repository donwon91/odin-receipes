Makin' a Hamburger

Everyone loves a good hamburger, and tonight we're gonna learn to make one!

Ingredients

  • 2 Buns. Or if you're an asshole, 2 large iceburg lettuce heads.
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Not Mustard
  • A chunk of bison ground meat

Puttin' it together

We realize some people kinda dumb, so here you are!

  1. Cook the bison to about medium or medium well.
  2. Once cooked, set aside and allow to rest.
  3. Take your buns and get them dressed with ketchup, mayo and whatever else.
  4. Put it altogether.
  5. Put it in your mouth hole.
