Seafood Pasta

Desription

This is a seafood pasta lover's dream! It's probably what I'm going to have for dinner/lunch tonight! Assuming I can find something affordable. It's gotta have a ton of seafood: muscles, clam, shrimp, calamari. basically, the whole ocean.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce of Choice
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemons
  • Muscles,Shrimp,Calamari, or whatever seafood
  • A Bowl

Steps to Create

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Put pasta in, until Al Dente
  3. Begin cooking seafood
  4. Using some of the starch water heat sauce
  5. Crush and sear garlic with seafood
  6. Mix all Ingredients
  7. Serve meal!
